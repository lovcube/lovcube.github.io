FAQ NON-WiFi version

  • Can the frame be used without the remote?

    Yes, once the settings are entered the remote is not needed. There are also buttons at the back that can be used in place of the remote. 

  • Does this frame "loop" after last photo is played?

    Yes, it shuffles or loops on a continuous play.

  • Is there a battery ?

    NO batteries; the frame needs to be connected to a power source. 

  • Is there internal memory ?

    No,there is no internal memory.
